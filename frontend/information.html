Health Awareness & Prevention

Early awareness and preventive care play a critical role in reducing the risk of chronic and life-threatening diseases. AI Health aims to promote informed decision-making by providing general guidance and risk insights.

General Preventive Guidelines

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Engage in regular physical activity to maintain a healthy weight.
  • Avoid tobacco and excessive alcohol consumption.
  • Monitor vital health parameters such as blood sugar and blood pressure.
  • Seek medical advice early if symptoms persist.

About AI-Based Risk Assessment

AI-based systems analyze patterns in medical data to identify potential risks. However, these systems do not replace doctors. They are designed to support early screening and encourage timely professional consultation.

All insights generated by AI Health should be interpreted as preliminary assessments.

This tool provides general health information and is not a medical diagnosis.
